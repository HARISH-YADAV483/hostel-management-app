Introduction

This platform has been specially developed for the welfare, support, and overall benefit of the students of Chakhardhar Bhawan. At National Institute of Technology, Kurukshetra, Chakhardhar Bhawan is officially known as Hostel Number H-3. For the academic session 2025‚Äì26, this hostel has been primarily allotted to first-year (fresher) students of the institute, helping them begin their college life in a safe, organized, and supportive residential environment. The hostel mainly accommodates students from the Information Technology (IT), Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Production Engineering, Artificial Intelligence and Data Science (AIDS), and Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AIML) branches.

This allocation encourages interaction and collaboration among students from closely related technical disciplines, promoting a healthy academic and social atmosphere. Chakhardhar Bhawan is structurally divided into two main blocks: Block A and Block B. Each block consists of three floors, and every floor contains fifteen well-planned rooms. Each room is designed to accommodate three students, ensuring comfortable living while encouraging teamwork and peer bonding. In total, the hostel has 90 rooms and can accommodate more than 250 students, making it one of the key residential facilities for freshmen at NIT Kurukshetra.
